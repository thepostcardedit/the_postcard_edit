About The Postcard Edit

The Postcard Edit began in 2013 as a visual project centered around documenting the beauty in everyday life. The quiet, the ordinary, the seemingly mundane. Over time, it became a recognisable micro-brand with a distinct aesthetic that focuses on simplicity, stillness, and emotional detail.

This website exists to clarify authorship and originality. The Postcard Edit is the original creator and originator of the name. Any accounts, profiles, or businesses using the same name were created at a later date.

What I Do

"Pretty pictures of the mundane" but deeper than that, I capture small moments with emotional weight. A personal lens on stillness, repetition, memory, and beauty hiding in plain sight.
